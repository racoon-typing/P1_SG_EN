2) The necessary set of exercises

Do workouts every other day, this will be the best option for your body. Do not overtrain yourself - 30-50 minutes of activity per day will be enough to strengthen muscles and start fat burning. You need moderate physical activity because hard sports do not affect your health in the best way, especially without proper training.

Warm-up:

  • 1) Jogging on the spot
  • 2) Bends over to the right and left
  • 3) Squats (10-15 times)
  • 4) Arbitrary hand swings

It is recommende to spend at least 5-8 minutes warming up before training.

Basic exercises:

1. Walking or running 5 km / Swimming 1 hour.

If you feel uncomfortable swimming, you can prioritize running or walking. It makes no sense to start walking regularly at marathon distances and at a fast pace. When walking becomes a joy, you can increase both the distance and the pace. Do not rush to increase the load - it is important not to scare yourself away from a good initiative. Two days of 5 km is better than a 15 km “race” and a complete rejection of the idea. Do not neglect walking as a regular exercise.

2. Twists — 2 sets, 10 reps

Straighten your arms above your head or put them behind your head. Exhale, tighten your abs, and hold your breath. Lift your head, shoulders, and shoulder blades off the floor, and leave your lower back pressed against it. Slowly return to the starting position and repeat.

3. Squats — 3 sets, 15 reps.

To do a squat, you need to keep your back straight. Then start slowly lowering your hips until they are parallel or almost parallel to the floor. When you are in the lower position, stop and hold the body for 1-2 seconds on exhalation, then return to the starting position.

4. Arm and leg lifts — 3 sets, 16 reps

Keeping your balance, straighten your back and at the same time slowly lift your left leg and right arm so that they are parallel to the floor. Pause, slowly return to the starting position, and repeat the exercise in the other direction. Do the exercise slowly, without inertia, only due to the work of the muscles.
